CHIKEN YAKHNI PULAO

Ingredients:

For Yakhni (Stock):
  • 500g chicken (bone-in pieces)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4 cloves
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 cups water
For Pulao:
  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
