STUFFED ALOO PARATHA

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 2 large boiled potatoes
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Ghee or oil for roasting

Instructions:

  1. Knead wheat flour into soft dough and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  2. Mash potatoes and mix with spices, chili, ginger, coriander.
  3. Divide dough into balls, stuff with potato filling, and roll into flat rounds.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa with ghee until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
