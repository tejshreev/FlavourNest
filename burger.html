CHIKEN BURGER

Ingredients:

For the patty:
  • 250g minced chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1‚Äì2 green chilies, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp oil
For assembling:
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 lettuce leaves
  • 4 cheese slices
  • 4 tomato slices
  • 4 onion rings
  • Mayonnaise and ketchup
  • Butter for toasting buns

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine minced chicken, onion, chilies, ginger-garlic paste, spices, bread crumbs, egg, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Divide into equal portions and shape into round patties.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and cook patties on medium flame until golden brown and fully cooked on both sides.
  4. Cut burger buns in half and lightly toast with butter.
  5. Spread mayonnaise and ketchup on the bottom bun, place lettuce, chicken patty, cheese slice, tomato, and onion.
  6. Cover with the top bun and serve hot with fries or salad.
