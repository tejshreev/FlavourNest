SURMAI FRY

Ingredients:

  • 4 surmai (kingfish) slices
  • 2 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup rice flour or semolina (for coating)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions:

  1. Clean the fish slices and pat them dry.
  2. In a bowl, mix red chili powder, turmeric powder, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and lemon juice to make a thick marinade.
  3. Coat the fish slices well with this marinade and let them rest for 20‚Äì30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a pan for shallow frying.
  5. Coat marinated fish in rice flour or semolina for a crispy texture.
  6. Fry each slice on medium flame for 3‚Äì4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with lemon wedges and onion rings.
