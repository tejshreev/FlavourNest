MOTICHUR LADOO

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • Water (as needed to make batter)
  • Ghee or oil (for deep frying)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water (for sugar syrup)
  • 2‚Äì3 cardamom pods (crushed)
  • 1/4 tsp orange food color (optional)
  • Chopped pistachios and almonds (for garnish)
  • 1 tsp rose water or kewra water (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Mix besan, baking soda, and enough water to make a flowing batter.
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a kadhai. Pour the batter using a slotted spoon to form tiny droplets (boondis).
  3. Fry until golden but soft. Remove and drain excess oil.
  4. In a separate pan, boil sugar and water to make a one-string syrup. Add cardamom and food color.
  5. Add fried boondis to the syrup and mix gently. Let them soak for 10‚Äì15 minutes.
  6. Shape into ladoos while slightly warm. Garnish with chopped nuts.
  7. Let them set and serve when firm. Store in an airtight container.
