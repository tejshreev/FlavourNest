FRIED PRAWNS

Ingredients:

  • 250g medium-sized prawns (peeled and deveined)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine cornflour, all-purpose flour, chili powder, turmeric, pepper, salt, lemon juice, and ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add a little water to make a thick batter.
  3. Coat the prawns well in the batter and let it marinate for 15‚Äì20 minutes.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Deep fry the prawns in batches until golden and crispy.
  6. Remove and place on a paper towel to drain excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with lemon wedges and mint chutney.
