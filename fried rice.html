PANEER FRIED RICE

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup paneer (cubed)
  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably chilled)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers (mixed colors, chopped)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (julienned)
  • 2-3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions:

  4. Add cooked rice and mix gently to combine with veggies.
  5. Add soy sauce, ketchup, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  6. Add the fried paneer cubes and stir lightly.
  7. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
