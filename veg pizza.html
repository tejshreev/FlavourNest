VEG PIZZA

Ingredients:

For the base:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water
For the topping:
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup capsicum, sliced
  • 1/2 cup onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup tomato, sliced
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn (optional)
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp chili flakes

Instructions:

  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into a soft dough. Cover and let it rise for 1‚Äì2 hours until doubled in size.

  6. Add sliced vegetables and sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Season with oregano and chili flakes.
  8. Bake for 12‚Äì15 minutes or until cheese is melted and crust is golden.
  9. Slice and serve hot.
