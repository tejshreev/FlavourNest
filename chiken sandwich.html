Chiken sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken (shredded)
  • 4 slices bread (white or brown)
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp mustard sauce (optional)
  • 1/4 cup lettuce (shredded)
  • 1/4 cup cucumber slices
  • 1/4 cup tomato slices
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Butter for toasting

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix shredded chicken with mayonnaise, mustard sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Spread butter on the bread slices and lightly toast them in a pan or toaster.
  3. Place lettuce on one slice of bread, followed by cucumber, tomato, and chicken mixture.
  4. Cover with the second slice of bread.
  5. Cut the sandwich into halves or quarters and serve immediately.
