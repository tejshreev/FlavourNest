OMLET

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp chopped onion
  • 1 tbsp chopped tomato
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp oil or butter

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk well.
  2. Add chopped onion, tomato, chili, coriander, salt, and pepper. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread evenly.

  6. Serve hot with bread toast, chutney, or as a side dish.
