FRESH FRUIT SALAD

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup diced apples
  • 1 cup diced kiwi
  • 1 cup diced bananas
  • 1 cup chopped pineapple
  • 1/2 cup grapes (halved)
  • 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 2 tbsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Chopped mint leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all chopped fruits.
  2. Drizzle with honey (or maple syrup) and lemon juice.
  3. Toss gently to coat fruits evenly.
  4. Chill for 15‚Äì20 minutes before serving.
  5. Garnish with fresh mint leaves and enjoy!
