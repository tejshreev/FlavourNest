MASALA DOSA

Ingredients:

For Dosa Batter:
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 3 cups rice
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for grinding and consistency)
For Masala (Potato Filling):
  • 3 large potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 6-8 curry leaves
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (chopped)

Instructions:

To Prepare Batter:
  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 5‚Äì6 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter, add salt, and ferment overnight or for 8‚Äì10 hours.
To Prepare Masala:
  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chili.
  2. Add sliced onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add turmeric, mashed potatoes, salt, and mix well.
  4. Cook for a few minutes and add chopped coriander. Set aside.
To Make Dosa:
  1. Heat a non-stick or cast-iron tawa. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it in circular motion.
  2. Drizzle a little oil around the edges and cook until golden and crisp.
  3. Place some potato masala in the center and fold the dosa.
  4. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
