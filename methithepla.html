healthy methi paratha

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup spinach (palak)or fenugreek leaves(methi)(washed and chopped)
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil or ghee for roasting

Instructions:

  1. Blanch spinach (if using) and puree it. If using methi, use as is.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, pureed greens, ginger-garlic paste, chili, cumin, turmeric, ajwain, and salt.
  3. Add little water and knead into a soft dough. Rest for 10‚Äì15 minutes.
  4. Roll into medium-sized parathas.
  5. Roast on hot tawa using little oil or ghee on both sides until golden.
  6. Serve hot with curd or pickle for a healthy meal!
