CHIKEN MOMOS

Ingredients:

  • 250 g minced chicken
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup spring onion greens
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Momo wrappers (or dough made from 1 cup all-purpose flour + water)
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix minced chicken, cabbage, onion, spring onion, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, black pepper, and salt.
  2. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each momo wrapper.
  3. Fold and seal the edges to form dumplings.
  4. Grease a steamer plate with oil and place momos on it.
  5. Steam for 10‚Äì12 minutes until cooked.
  6. Serve hot with spicy chutney.
