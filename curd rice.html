DAHI RICE

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cooled)
  • 1 cup fresh curd (yogurt)
  • 1-2 green chilies (chopped)
  • 1/2 inch ginger (grated)
  • 1/4 cup coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 dried red chilies
  • 8-10 curry leaves
  • 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk curd until smooth and add salt.
  2. Add the cooled rice to the curd and mix well. Set aside.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and let them splutter.
  4. Add red chilies, curry leaves, green chilies, ginger, and asafoetida. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  5. Pour this tempering over the curd rice and mix well.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve chilled or at room temperature.
