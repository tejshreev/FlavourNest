COLD COFFEE

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chilled milk
  • 2 tbsp instant coffee powder
  • 2 tbsp sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 scoops vanilla or chocolate ice cream
  • Chocolate syrup (for garnish)
  • Whipped cream (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, add chilled milk, coffee powder, sugar, and 1 scoop of ice cream.
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. Drizzle chocolate syrup inside the serving glass for decoration.
  4. Pour the blended cold coffee into the glass.
  5. Top with a scoop of ice cream and whipped cream.
  6. Garnish with more chocolate syrup and serve chilled.
