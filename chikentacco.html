CHIKEN TACCO

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked and shredded chicken (grilled or boiled)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 small taco shells (soft or crunchy)
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar or mozzarella)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions (optional)
  • Sour cream or taco sauce (for topping)
  • Fresh coriander or cilantro leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat olive oil and add shredded chicken.
  2. Sprinkle chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Mix well and saut√© for 5‚Äì7 minutes until the chicken is well coated and slightly crisp.
  4. Warm the taco shells on a pan or in the oven for a few seconds.
  5. Fill each taco shell with cooked chicken, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and cheese.
  6. Top with sour cream or taco sauce and garnish with fresh coriander.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
