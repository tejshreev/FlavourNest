SOYA CUTTLET

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup soya chunks
  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour or bread crumbs (for binding)
  • Bread crumbs (for coating)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions:

  1. Soak soya chunks in hot water for 10 minutes. Drain and squeeze out excess water, then grind coarsely.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground soya, mashed potatoes, onion, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste, and spices.
  3. Add cornflour or bread crumbs for binding and mix well.
  4. Shape the mixture into cutlets and coat with bread crumbs.
  5. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry cutlets until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Serve hot with chutney or ketchup.
