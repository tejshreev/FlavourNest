CHEESE CAKE WITH CHEERY TOPING

Ingredients:

For the crust:
  • 200g digestive biscuits, crushed
  • 100g unsalted butter, melted
For the cheesecake filling:
  • 500g cream cheese, softened
  • 200g powdered sugar
  • 200ml fresh cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
For the cherry topping:
  • 1 cup fresh or canned cherries
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp cornflour mixed with 2 tbsp water

Instructions:

  1. Mix crushed biscuits with melted butter. Press firmly into the base of a springform pan to form the crust. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add powdered sugar, vanilla extract, lemon juice, and mix well.
  3. Whip fresh cream until soft peaks form, then fold into the cream cheese mixture.
  4. Pour the filling over the chilled crust and smooth the top. Refrigerate for at least 4‚Äì5 hours or overnight.
  5. For the topping: Heat cherries with sugar and lemon juice in a pan. Once it starts to simmer, add the cornflour slurry and stir until the mixture thickens. Let it cool completely.
  6. Pour the cooled cherry topping over the set cheesecake before serving.
