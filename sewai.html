SEWAI KHEER

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup roasted vermicelli (sewai)
  • 1 liter full cream milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 2-3 cardamom pods (crushed)
  • 10-12 cashews
  • 10-12 almonds (sliced)
  • 10-12 raisins
  • A few saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and roast cashews, almonds, and raisins. Remove and keep aside.
  2. In the same pan, roast the vermicelli until golden brown.
  3. In another pot, bring milk to a boil.
  4. Add roasted vermicelli to the milk and cook on low flame until soft.
  5. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and saffron strands. Mix well.
  6. Cook for another 5‚Äì7 minutes until slightly thickened.
  7. Garnish with roasted nuts and serve warm or chilled.
